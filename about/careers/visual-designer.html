      Blow users away with jaw-dropping designs

      At Yummygum we make top notch digital solutions for amazing clients at spectacular companies, big and small. From our clean and minimal office located in the heart of Amsterdam we refine, design and build brands and digital products.

      Send us your portfolio!
      • A tightknit team of focused individuals
      • Full time (that’s 35hrs for us)
      • Continuous growth framework
      • Relaxed office hours
      • Employee bicycle scheme
      • Travel allowance
      • 40h salary €2319 - €3299

      As a Visual Designer at Yummygum you'll be kicking it with the rest of the visual design squad doing visual designery things. But in all seriousness your team is responsible for making sure everything looks beautiful AND usable.

      You'll be working on designing the most gorgeous web apps, mobile apps, websites, icons, illustrations and even get your hands dirty with some motion.

      You'll spend your day picking up tasks in Linear and designing in Figma. And of course meme'ing around on Slack. You're in direct contact with our clients. This means finding the right words to say why you made such and such design decision is key. Not just towards the client but also the rest of the team.

      Together we'll be focussing on setting up a personal growth framework, encouraging and facilitating you to grow in the things you love doing.

      Our tools of choice

      • Figma

      • Slack

      • Linear

      • Notion

      • Adobe Creative Cloud

      What are you capable of?

      You know your way around in Figma like no other. Working with Components and Styles is something you’re very familiar with. You are aware of how design decisions, like a certain color or corner radius, affects the look and feel of a design. You also have no problem transforming wireframes into finger-licking, pixel perfect designs.

      You’re intrigued by creating beautiful designs that are consistent and usable. You know how to put your design decisions into words and love getting inspired by team mates.

      Working 8px Grids is something you’re comfortable with and you’ve heard about Atomic Design. Perhaps you’ve even fiddle’d around with applying in your own designs. You fully understand there are certain platform sizing and conventions and patterns. And you keep these in mind when you’re designing for iOS, Android or web.

      Ideally you've completed a study that’s closely related to everything we mentioned above. Communication & Multimedia Design or Media College to name a few. You absolutely love designing and you have a portfolio that exists of more than just what was asked from you from your study.

      In short, you'll

      • Help set up & use design systems with atomic design

      • Create beautiful icons and illustrations

      • Elaborate and explain your designs to the client

      • Occasionally whip out After Effects for motion design

      You are..

      • Great in Dutch & English language

      • Know your way around Figma

      • A proud owner of a portfolio to show your past work
