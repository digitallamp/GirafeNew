      Go by one dresscode; wear your heart on your sleeve

      As new industries surface and existing ones saturate you'll want to stand out. Cue 'brand'. We help you build a memorable brand. One that tells your story. One that set expectations. One that helps you create relations with your audience and keep them strong.

      First let's address the elephant in the room; what is 'brand'? Simply put, your brand is how people think of you and feel about you. Perhaps you've never thought about your business as a brand. Or maybe you have but it slipped away in the midst of expanding your team and building your product.

      Whatever the case may be, our branding team is here to help. We start by asking the hard questions that really matter. During a Brand Sprint we get your team aligned on your vision and mission. We look for what makes your business unique and turn our findings into a brand concept; a one-of-a-kind mixture of a metaphorical theme and elements like color, typeface, shape, tone of voice, and to top it off a logo.

      The only thing we'll ask from you is to show your true colors. See, we believe you should not need to pretend to be something you're not. Your (potential) audience sees right through it. But lucky for us, your audience knows when your brand is authentic. We help you find the right words and hand you a microphone to amplify your brand message.
